How do I see the differences between HTML and CSS?

  1. What is the difference between the usage of HTML and CSS?

    Quite simply, HTML (Hypertext Markup Language) is used to create the actual content of the page, such as written text, and CSS (Cascade Styling Sheets) is responsible for the design or style of the website, including the layout, visual effects and background color.

    A semantic element clearly describes its meaning to both the browser and the developer. non-semantic elements: "div" and "span" tell nothing about its content.
